Title: Upgrade guide

Position: 4

Content:

1. Make a full backup, all files and directories.
2. Download the latest version from https://s3.amazonaws.com/bludit-s3/bludit-builds/bludit_latest.zip
3. Extract the zip file.
4. Replace existing files with the new files.
5. *Log into the admin area*.
6. Done

<div markdown="1" class="note">
<div class="note-title">IMPORTANT!</div>
After the upgrade, it's highly recommended to check your settings.
</div>

<div markdown="1" class="note">
<div class="note-title">NOTE</div>
You can use a FTP client to upload files to the hosting. Check this page [FTP](../misc/ftp)
</div>
