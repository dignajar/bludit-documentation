Title: How to make a post
Position: 1
Content:

Log in to your Admin panel and go to **New post** on the top menu.

Complete the form, write a title and the content for the new post, then click on the button **Save**.

![Bludit new post](https://s3.amazonaws.com/bludit-s3/bludit-documentation/en/bludit-new-post-en.png)
